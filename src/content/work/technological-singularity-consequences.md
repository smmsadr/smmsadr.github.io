Originally written for Programming for Everybody (Python) on Coursera, this essay argues that intelligence is not outside scientific law and considers whether technology could eventually reach a point comparable to human intelligence.

The original article discusses the possibility of singularity through computers, quantum computers, and developments in neuroscience.
