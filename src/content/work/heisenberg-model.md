This B.Sc. project, supervised by Prof. Abdollah Langari at Sharif University of Technology, studied eigenvalues and eigenvectors of a finite chain with spin particles in the Heisenberg model.

The older summary describes closed-chain eigenfunctions, renormalization for interaction between chains at zero temperature, and fixed points from effective renormalization energy.
