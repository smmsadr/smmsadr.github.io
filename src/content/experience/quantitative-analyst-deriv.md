## Context

Deriv (formerly Binary Group Services) operates a global online
trading platform. This is where I made the jump from running Linux
infrastructure to building quantitative analyses — inside the same
company, on the same data I had previously kept flowing.

## Highlights

- Built quantitative workflows for decision support across trading
  and risk-oriented analytics.
- Contributed to production analytics, model interpretation, and the
  measurement of live system behaviour.
- Bridged operational data — the systems I had previously
  administered — with the analytical layer serving trading and risk
  teams.

## Reflection

The pivot role. Being able to look at trading data with an operations
mindset (what actually happens at 3am when the system misbehaves?)
made the analyses more grounded. It also confirmed that the future
was on the modelling side.
