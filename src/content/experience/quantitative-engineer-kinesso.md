## Context

A short but focused stretch inside Kinesso's technology group,
bridging the earlier modelling analyst work with the senior data
scientist role that followed. The remit expanded from building
individual models to hardening the production analytics stack behind
cross-channel planning.

## Highlights

- Enhanced planning models for budget allocation and media performance
  optimization, tightening the objective formulations and solver
  configurations behind the existing tools.
- Improved TV and OOH (out-of-home) audience measurement workflows
  with probabilistic modelling and optimization-based reconciliation
  approaches.
- Delivered solver-backed analytics services in Python, integrated
  with the cloud data platforms feeding the wider planning stack.

## Reflection

The transition role that shifted my mindset from writing a good model
to owning a well-behaved production service around it — logging,
retries, timeouts, and clear failure semantics for the teams
downstream.
