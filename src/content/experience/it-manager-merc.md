## Context

The Materials and Energy Research Center is a public research
organization. As IT Manager I led the operations that kept researchers
and administrative staff productive — infrastructure, service support,
user-facing systems, and everything in between.

## Highlights

- Oversaw IT operations across systems administration, service
  support, and the day-to-day plumbing behind the organization's
  scientific and administrative work.
- Coordinated infrastructure requirements across multiple research
  groups and administrative units, translating scientific workflows
  into technical requirements.
- Introduced Linux and structured network-service practices across
  research units — the operational foundations that would carry over
  to every later role.

## Reflection

The beginning of the systems thinking journey. Managing IT for a
research organization taught me that the interesting problems live
in the interactions — between users, workflows, hardware, and
software — not in any single tool. That framing has stayed with me
through every role since.
