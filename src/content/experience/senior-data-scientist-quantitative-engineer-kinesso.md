## Context

Kinesso sits inside IPG Mediabrands, delivering media planning and
performance technology to global brands. My work is with the
international engineering and product teams that build the
optimization and AI systems clients across multiple markets rely on
every day to allocate budget, plan campaigns, and reason about
audiences.

## Highlights

- Architected and shipped production-grade optimization engines —
  Budget Allocation, Outcome Planner — built on Pyomo, GAMS, CPLEX,
  IPOPT and orchestrated on scalable cloud data workflows.
- Delivered end-to-end machine learning and generative-AI
  capabilities including multi-armed bandit bidding, semantic
  audience search, Bayesian audience modelling, and LLM-based
  planning assistants (LangChain, LangGraph, Gemini).
- Operationalized model + solver services behind FastAPI, integrated
  with the group's cloud data platforms and delivery pipelines.
- Contributed to capability building inside Malaysia-based technology
  teams by advancing adoption of mathematical optimization, hybrid
  AI, and generative AI practices.
- Partnered with product, engineering, and analytics leaders across
  markets to translate business objectives into rigorous
  decision-support systems.

## Reflection

What this role taught me: model quality alone doesn't win — models
have to become products. Reliability, observability, and the humility
to expose uncertainty to decision makers are what turn a good model
into a platform teams actually trust.
