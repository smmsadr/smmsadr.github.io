## Context

Joined what was then IPG Mediabrands Technologies (rebranded to
Kinesso Malaysia) as a full-time quantitative modelling analyst.
The team was standing up the analytical foundations that later
became the group's global planning stack, and my job was to build the
models under it.

## Highlights

- Developed foundational audience and media-response models used
  across strategic planning workflows, from reach curves to
  frequency-effect estimation.
- Contributed to marketing mix modelling (MMM) and omnichannel
  optimization initiatives measuring how spend translated into
  campaign effectiveness across markets.
- Improved audience measurement pipelines with probabilistic
  modelling for source reconciliation and target-group estimation.
- Built solver-backed analytics services that plugged model outputs
  into the internal planning tools — the first version of the loop
  that later evolved into a full production platform.
- Established repeatable analytical pipelines over large datasets so
  models could be re-estimated and shipped without hand-holding.

## Reflection

Where I learned that in decision systems, the model is the
easy part — the hard parts are the data plumbing behind it and the
usability of its output on the other side.
