## Context

An eight-month contract engagement (February to October 2018) through
Experis, part of ManpowerGroup, working with the same IPG Mediabrands
Technologies team I would later join full-time as Kinesso Malaysia.
The remit was to plug directly into ongoing quantitative modelling
delivery on a rapid turnaround — no ramp-up runway, just outcomes.

## Highlights

- Delivered audience and media-response model iterations across
  active planning workstreams, working to the client team's release
  cadence rather than a research timeline.
- Contributed end-to-end to planning-focused quantitative workflows:
  data preparation, model fitting, evaluation, and packaging outputs
  for internal consumers.
- Supported the reporting and analytics layer that surfaced model
  outputs into the planning tools the client team already used.
- Bridged operational data sources into planning-oriented analytical
  outputs, keeping downstream model consumers unblocked while the
  permanent team scaled.
- Earned the trust that translated directly into a full-time offer
  at Kinesso Malaysia later that year — the same analytical work,
  now with runway to invest in the platform underneath it.

## Reflection

The gateway into media analytics. Eight months of proving that a
short, well-scoped contract can be the fastest way to earn a seat at
the table — and that the way you deliver under time pressure is the
best interview an organisation will ever run.
